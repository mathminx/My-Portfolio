About Me

I was born in the UK and raised in a small town just outside Toronto. I have a BA in economics from the University of Waterloo, and an MSc in Secondary Mathematics Education from Niagara University in Lewiston, NY. Before becoming a teacher, I worked in the financial industry for over 20 years. I am certified to teach high school math in Ontario and New Brunswick, where I now live with my husband, Jason, and our two cats. I recently completed the University of New Brunswick Coding Boot Camp, earning a certificate in full-stack web development. I plan to combine my newly-acquired tech skills with my creativity and flair for design to become a front-end developer.
